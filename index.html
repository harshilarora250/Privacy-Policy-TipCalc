Last Updated: September 28, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Tip Calculator app ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

Key Point: Our Tip Calculator app is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or calculation data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Our Tip Calculator app is designed to operate entirely on your device without collecting any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal Information: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any identifying information
  • Calculation Data: Bill amounts, tip percentages, or splitting information you enter
  • Usage Analytics: How you use the app, frequency of use, or feature preferences
  • Device Information: Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data
  • Financial Information: No payment methods, credit card information, or financial data

How the App Works

The Tip Calculator app operates entirely offline on your device:

  • All calculations are performed locally on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No internet connection is required for the app to function
  • All entered information is temporary and not saved between app sessions

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there are no data security concerns related to our servers or databases. However, we ensure:

  • The app operates in a secure, sandboxed environment on your device
  • No sensitive information is transmitted or stored
  • Regular security updates through app store updates
  • Adherence to mobile platform security guidelines

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. This includes:

  • No Google Analytics or similar tracking services
  • No social media integrations
  • No advertising networks or ad tracking
  • No cloud storage or backup services

Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special privacy concerns for younger users. Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is protected when using our Tip Calculator app.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
